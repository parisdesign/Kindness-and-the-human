Kindness and the human

Gameplay

Once upon a time, the world was protected by the mother plants who loved everyone, they held the world with their roots and created vegetation for all life forms to survive on.  Tragedy struck and humans, greedy as they are, took the mother plants in the hopes of using their intelligence and seeds for their comfort. Their experiments succeeded, but produced vengeful monsters that were bent on destroying everything...

Filled with anger and sadness, thinking about how much they gave and what was taken, the monsters shattered the world. There was no forgiveness, no kindness, and no hope… 

But there is still you! Be their hero, Kindness, who’ll save everyone and restore life to the world. Uncover the story, find out about your past, and turn up the challenge by speedrunning the game!
